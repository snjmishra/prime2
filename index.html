Equipment's & Technology

We have the latest & the most specialized 3D Design software’s, Project management resources , experienced & dedicated Vendor base for building plant with the latest Technologies in Surface Coating Systems, i.e. Liquid painting, Powder coating, Cathodic Electro – Deposition (CED) , Aquence®, Fusion Bonded Epoxy (FBE) Coating Plants, etc. Our Paint Shops are designed to meet “FM Global standards”.
